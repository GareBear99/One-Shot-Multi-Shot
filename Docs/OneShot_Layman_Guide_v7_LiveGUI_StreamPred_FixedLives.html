One Shot — One‑Click Direction Trading (Guide)

A simple UP/DOWN predictor that updates every 5 seconds, with a live confidence meter updating every 1 second.
Archived: 2025-12-31 • Mode: Demo UI
Plain English: One Shot shows a direction (UP/DOWN). You pick UP or DOWN, choose a stake ($0–$100), and an expiry. If you guess right, you receive your stake back plus a fixed profit. If wrong, you lose the stake. (Payout rates depend on the venue you integrate with.)

How scoring works (no cheating)
Each prediction is locked at time t. After the expiry H (example: 60s), we compare price at t+H to price at t.

If price went up → UP prediction counts as a win. If price went down → DOWN prediction counts as a win.

3) Quick Start (3 steps)

Step 1: Watch the signal for a minute. Make sure win rates are strong and the confidence meter is stable.

Step 2: Choose UP or DOWN, set your stake (0–100), and pick an expiry.
Step 3: Click Place One Shot Trade. After expiry, the system grades your trade and updates the win‑rate history.
Disclaimers: This UI is a documentation demo. Real trading requires integration with a compliant exchange/broker or DeFi execution and may require KYC/AML. This is not financial advice and does not guarantee results.

OneShot Auto Rules (Locked)

Auto enable: requires detected balance ≥ $15.
Daily lives: 3 hearts (3 sequential $5 attempts). No parallel attempts.
Early growth: only P + S trades (A-class disabled).
Stake ladder: automatic scaling from $1 to $20 with your balance bands.

With payout +80% on a win:
EV = W*(0.8*stake) - (1-W)*stake = (1.8W - 1)*stake

How it works (Live wiring plan)
Production architecture: UI doesn't compute trades. It consumes a stream of events: STATE, PREDICTION, TRADE_RESULT, HEARTS.

Adapters:
  • DemoAdapter (this page): generates mock events and respects your rules
  • WsAdapter: subscribe to a backend WebSocket that pushes events
  • LogReplayAdapter: replay real logs for debugging + marketing demos
Refresh loops: confidence UI at 1s, feed refresh at 5s. In live mode, these are just render timers; the data arrives event-driven.
Key Safety Guarantees
• Worst-case daily loss cap = $15 (3 failed $5 attempts).
• Attempts are sequential (no parallel “rotations”).
• Early growth uses P/S only to reduce wipe probability.
• Stake ladder is automatic and bounded: $1–$20.
